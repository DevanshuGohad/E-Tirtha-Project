Tirupati Balaji Temple

Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India

About the Temple

The Tirupati Balaji Temple, also known as Sri Venkateswara Temple, is one of the most visited temples in the world. Located in the hill town of Tirumala, it is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.

Historical Significance

The temple has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It is mentioned in various Puranas and has been a significant pilgrimage site for centuries. The temple's architecture reflects the Dravidian style and has been expanded over time by various dynasties.

Temple Architecture

The temple complex features a magnificent gopuram (tower) and several mandapams (halls). The main temple is built in Dravidian architecture with intricate carvings and sculptures. The temple's inner sanctum houses the sacred idol of Lord Venkateswara.
